Placement Procedure

To support the placement process, the Training and Placement Cell has an effective system in place. The procedure for campus/pool campus placement follows the following plan:

Initiation

  1. The Training & Placement Officer sends invitations to companies/organizations along with relevant information.
  2. The company/organization reverts with a filled Job Announcement Form (JAF) containing details of recruitment process, job description, package (CTC), location, allowances & other benefits.
  3. JAFs can be sent either by post or email to the Training & Placement Cell, Edu IIT Ambala (tpo@eduiitambala.ac.in).
  4. Slot for the visit of the company/organization for recruitment is decided upon a mutual agreement.
  5. If the company wishes to conduct a Pre-Placement Talk (PPT), they can send a request with preferred dates.
  6. Additional information such as company profile, past hiring trends, and required skill sets are also requested to prepare students effectively.

Selection

  1. Companies conduct their selection process which may include Aptitude Test, Group Discussion, Technical Interview, and HR Interview.
  2. Students are informed about the selection stages well in advance to ensure preparation.
  3. Results are shared with the Training & Placement Cell for announcement.

Post-Selection

  1. Selected students receive offer letters either directly or through the Training & Placement Cell.
  2. The Cell maintains a database of selected candidates and coordinates with recruiters for joining formalities.
  3. Feedback from recruiters is collected to improve future placement activities.
